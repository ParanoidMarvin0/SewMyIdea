About

Jeanniene is making sewing cool again with practical classes that focus on building foundational skills that can be applied to any project. She loves sharing her sewing skills and experience with anyone willing to learn!

Jeanniene first learned to sew more than 20 years ago, as a student in the kids can sew program. Since then, she continued to build her skills by drawing patterns, and sewing up her ideas that varied from formal dresses and everyday clothes to a collection of purses, drapery, and re-upholstery. In 2014 she developed an adult sewing curriculum - SewMyIdea, and started teaching at the Creative City Centre in downtown Regina. Building on student feedback, her classes have grown to include multiple levels, field trips, and workshops!
